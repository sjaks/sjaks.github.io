Rice

I think one's operating system is no more than a tool but it's important to have good and powerful tools.

Modifying your desktop and other parts of your system to your liking is quite important to me. While it being a nice addition, changing only the visuals is not the cool part. But configuring your text editor, window manager among others, is really benefitical and fun.

I used to use i3wm but I have noticed that Gnome 3.36 is just fine and focusing on tweaking other parts of the system is more important.
